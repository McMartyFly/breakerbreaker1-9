What We Do

Breaker Breaker One Nine (BB19) was born out of a keen interest among truckers to improve the information and tools available to professionals on the road. Travis Pope, a professional trucker for 23 years, led a team of dedicated truckers in determining the best set of tools to address the needs of the average trucking professional. What resulted is our target for BB19. While Travis directs the professional trucking team, others work in the back office to get the technology, customer service and marketing to fall into place. All in all, Breaker Breaker 1-9, Inc. is dedicated to making the best app for all truckers
